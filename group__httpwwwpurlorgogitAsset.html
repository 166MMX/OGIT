ogit/Asset

Assets represent a physical hold of a value and are situated in a location. Assets could correspond to one or more ogit/Automation/MARSNode's and can be physical or virtual. Assets of a Service Provider include anything that could contribute to the delivery of a Service. Assets can have a type, for example: Management, Organisation, Process, Knowledge, People, Information, Applications, Infrastructure, Financial Capital.
